Welcome!

Are you looking to learn how to use Krita to paint digitally? If so, you've come to the right place! This tutorial will teach you the basics of Krita by guiding you through a painting demo.

Scroll to begin!

If you prefer to learn Krita's tools by reading about them, click on any of the links to the right to learn the basics about a specific tool.

Painting Demo

This is a step-by-step guide on how to paint a wolf in Krita. I will show you all the tools I use and teach you the basics of Krita starting from creating a document. I encourage you to follow along, and you can post your final painting for others to see!
